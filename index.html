ICDM 2026 Workshop

Human Dynamics and Mobility Analytics in Agentic AI Era

HDMA 2026 brings together researchers and practitioners working on human mobility, broader human dynamics, spatio-temporal data intelligence, and agentic AI systems.
Workshop Abbreviation: HDMA 2026
Format: Half-day or Full-day
Location: ICDM 2026
View Call for Papers Contact Us

Overview

Human mobility data, together with broader human behavioral data generated from online platforms and human–AI interactions, provide rich signals for understanding human dynamics in physical and digital environments. HDMA 2026 focuses on the intersection of human dynamics analytics and emerging agentic AI paradigms, with the goal of advancing modeling, reasoning, decision-making, and real-world applications in this area.

Research Focus

Human mobility, human behavior modeling, urban computing, spatio-temporal intelligence, and AI systems.

Why Now

Large models and agentic AI create new opportunities for adaptive reasoning over complex human-centered data.

Workshop Goal

Build a forum for discussing recent advances, open challenges, and real-world applications.

Call for Papers

We invite original research contributions that advance the understanding, modeling, and utilization of human mobility data and broader human dynamics in the emerging landscape of large models and agentic AI.

Topics of Interest

  • Modeling and understanding of human mobility
  • Mobility forecasting and anomaly detection
  • Multi-agent simulation of mobility, interaction, diffusion, coordination, and population dynamics
  • Representation learning for mobility trajectories
  • Agentic reasoning, planning, and tool use over multimodal human-centered data
  • Large models for human mobility data understanding
  • Mobility foundation models and large mobility datasets
  • Agentic AI for mobility reasoning and decision making
  • Retrieval-augmented, memory-augmented, and long-context systems for behavior mining
  • Human-in-the-loop agents for decision support, recommendation, and intervention design
  • Applications in health, smart cities, transportation, workplaces, education, disaster response, and online platforms
